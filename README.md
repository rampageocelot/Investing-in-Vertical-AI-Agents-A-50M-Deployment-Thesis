I recently explored the Agentic AI Market, Emphasizing on Voice agents. 

The primary question being, If I had 50M$ to invest today into Agentic AI, Where and how would I do it.

Here are the top 3 takeaways.

Here are three one-line takeaways:

1. AI agents are becoming the enterprise execution layer, moving beyond copilots to fully automate multi-step workflows.

2. The strongest defensibility comes from vertical agentic systems built around deep integrations, compliance, and domain-specific processes.

3. Government and legal represent a $3B greenfield opportunity where agentic AI can immediately replace manual, document-heavy case workflows.
